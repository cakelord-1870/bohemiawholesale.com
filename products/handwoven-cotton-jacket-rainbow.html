    Full Description

    Our unisex Handwoven Cotton Jacket in rainbow stripe is an easy to wear and flattering relaxed shape, this jacket wraps at the front to tie with a cotton belt in the same fabric and features two lined side pockets.

    Handwoven on traditional wooden looms in Turkey, where weaving is an ancestral craft passed down through the generations, our natural cotton jackets can be matched with a hammam towel in the same fabric to make a great travel set.

    • 100% Cotton
    • Handwoven and made in Turkey
    • One size
    • Unisex
    • Bust/width approx 66cm
    • Length approx 79cm
    • Sleeve length approx 55cm
    • Tie length approx 186cm

    Shipping and Returns

    DELIVERY

    We are still working with a reduced team and following government advice on keeping our warehouse and staff safe during the pandemic.

    UK Mainland - a carriage charge of £10 applies to orders under £500 (ex VAT), carriage is free on orders above £500 (ex VAT).
