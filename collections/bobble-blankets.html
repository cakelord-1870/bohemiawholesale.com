    Our vibrant pom pom blankets are a traditional Berber design with a Bohemia twist. Available in our bespoke colours, the cotton is hand dyed and woven into cosy contemporary blankets with oversized pom poms or tassels. Our exclusive Scribble Stripe blankets are designed in our Edinburgh studio and handwoven on traditional wooden looms by expert weavers in Morocco. Made using time honoured techniques, our modern bohemian blankets make great bed covers or throws for sofas and chairs.
