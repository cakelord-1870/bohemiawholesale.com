    The Samburu collection is a new collaboration between Bohemia Design and BeadWORKS Kenya.The collection features vibrant statement beaded necklaces and bold tassel earrings, inspired by traditional African beadwork. It is entirely handmade using quality Czech glass beads and nickel free silver plated earwires. This exciting project helps support a virtuous circle, empowering women and protecting wildlife. BeadWORKS is a social enterprise working with self-governed women's groups in rural communities spread across northern Kenya. It provides a sustainable income for over 1,300 women. Learn more about BeadWORKS and our Creative Process.
