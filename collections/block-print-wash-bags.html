    Washbags

    Featuring graphic prints in a range of colours, our block printed canvas washbags are the perfect travel accessory. Designed in our Edinburgh studio the washbags are ethically handmade in a small artisan workshop in Rajasthan, India where block printing has been carried out since the 16th century. Lined with waterproof material our wash bags make excellent cosmetic or makeup bag, large enough to fit all your essentials.
